Reasoning and Findings

DuckDB is often fastest for single-machine analytical loops due to vectorized execution and low orchestration overhead. Custom PySpark can be slower locally because of RDD shuffle/serialization overhead, but it reflects distributed execution structure. GraphFrames simplifies implementation and can be competitive, though it still inherits Spark job overhead and graph construction costs. Rank order should broadly agree across methods, with small numeric differences caused by implementation details and floating-point accumulation.
